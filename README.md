This Python script logs into your Roblox account, fetches all badges associated with it, and attempts to delete them automatically. The script is fully customizable and has been tested extensively.

I’ve personally used it on 20+ accounts without any bans or warnings, but you should still use it at your own risk. Roblox may change their systems or policies at any time, and I am not responsible for any actions taken against your account.

## Features

* Automatically fetches all badges from an account
* Bulk badge deletion
* Customizable settings and delays
* CSRF/token handling
* Rate-limit handling improvements
